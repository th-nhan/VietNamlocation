✨ DISCOVER HOI AN - 3 DAYS 2 NIGHTS TOUR ✨

🏯 Explore the Ancient Beauty of Hoi An! 🏯

🌏 Departure from Ho Chi Minh City
📅 Tour Dates: March 15 - March 17, 2024
💰 Only $139

Join our amazing 3-day, 2-night tour to Hoi An, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and experience its charming old town, vibrant lantern-lit streets, and stunning beaches!

🌟 Tour Itinerary

📅 Day 1: March 15, 2024 - Ho Chi Minh City → Da Nang → Hoi An
✈️ Morning: Flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Da Nang
🏝️ Afternoon: Visit My Khe Beach - one of Vietnam's most beautiful beaches
🏯 Explore Marble Mountains - a breathtaking natural wonder
🚗 Transfer to Hoi An, check-in at a 3-star hotel in the city center
🌙 Evening: Enjoy a romantic walk through Hoi An Ancient Town, admire colorful lanterns, and taste local street food
📅 Day 2: March 16, 2024 - Hoi An Exploration
🚣‍♂️ Morning: Visit Bay Mau Coconut Village, enjoy a fun basket boat ride
🎨 Join a lantern-making workshop and craft your own souvenir
🍜 Afternoon: Try local specialties like Cao Lầu, Bánh Mì, and Bánh Xèo
🌆 Evening: Free time to explore Hoi An's night market, take photos, and enjoy the beautiful riverside view
📅 Day 3: March 17, 2024 - Hoi An → Da Nang → Ho Chi Minh City
⛩️ Morning: Visit Japanese Covered Bridge, an iconic symbol of Hoi An
🛕 Explore Phuc Kien Assembly Hall and ancient houses
🚗 Transfer back to Da Nang, relax at Dragon Bridge
✈️ Afternoon: Flight back to Ho Chi Minh City
