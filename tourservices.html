✨ DISCOVER HOI AN - 3 DAYS 2 NIGHTS TOUR ✨

🏯 Explore the Ancient Beauty of Hoi An! 🏯

🌏 Departure from Ho Chi Minh City
📅 Tour Dates: March 15 - March 17, 2025
💰 Only $139

Join our amazing 3-day, 2-night tour to Hoi An, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and experience its charming old town, vibrant lantern-lit streets, and stunning beaches!

🌟 Tour Itinerary

📅 Day 1: March 15, 2025 - Ho Chi Minh City → Da Nang → Hoi An
✈️ Morning: Flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Da Nang
🏝️ Afternoon: Visit My Khe Beach - one of Vietnam's most beautiful beaches
🏯 Explore Marble Mountains - a breathtaking natural wonder
🚗 Transfer to Hoi An, check-in at a 3-star hotel in the city center
🌙 Evening: Enjoy a romantic walk through Hoi An Ancient Town, admire colorful lanterns, and taste local street food
📅 Day 2: March 16, 2025 - Hoi An Exploration
🚣‍♂️ Morning: Visit Bay Mau Coconut Village, enjoy a fun basket boat ride
🎨 Join a lantern-making workshop and craft your own souvenir
🍜 Afternoon: Try local specialties like Cao Lầu, Bánh Mì, and Bánh Xèo
🌆 Evening: Free time to explore Hoi An's night market, take photos, and enjoy the beautiful riverside view
📅 Day 3: March 17, 2025 - Hoi An → Da Nang → Ho Chi Minh City
⛩️ Morning: Visit Japanese Covered Bridge, an iconic symbol of Hoi An
🛕 Explore Phuc Kien Assembly Hall and ancient houses
🚗 Transfer back to Da Nang, relax at Dragon Bridge
✈️ Afternoon: Flight back to Ho Chi Minh City

🏨 Accommodation:
✅ Stay at a 3-star hotel in Hoi An with full amenities

✔️ Professional & friendly tour guides

✨THAILAND DISCOVERY - 6 DAYS 5 NIGHTS TOUR✨

🌏 Departure from: Ho Chi Minh City
📅 Tour Date: April 10 - April 15, 2025
💰 Price: $399/person - All-inclusive package!

✨ Highlights:
✅ Bangkok - The vibrant capital with grand temples & shopping streets
✅ Pattaya - Stunning beaches & exciting nightlife
✅ Ayutthaya - Ancient city with rich history & culture
✅ Floating Market - Unique shopping experience on water
✅ Phi Phi Islands - Tropical paradise with crystal-clear waters

🌟 Tour Itinerary

📅 DAY 1 (April 10, 2024) | Ho Chi Minh City → Bangkok ✈️
Morning: Flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Bangkok
Afternoon: Visit Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha)
Evening: Enjoy a dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya River, admiring Bangkok's night skyline
Hotel: 4-star hotel in Bangkok
📅 DAY 2 (April 11, 2025) | Bangkok - Ayutthaya - Pattaya 🚗
Morning: Travel to Ayutthaya, explore:
✔️ Wat Mahathat - The famous Buddha head entwined in tree roots
✔️ Wat Phra Si Sanphet - The grand royal temple
Afternoon: Transfer to Pattaya, check in to a beachside hotel
Evening: Free time to explore Pattaya's Walking Street or relax on the beach
Hotel: 4-star beach resort in Pattaya
📅 DAY 3 (April 12, 2025) | Pattaya - Coral Island 🏝️
Morning: Speedboat ride to Coral Island, enjoy:
✔️ Snorkeling & swimming in crystal-clear waters
✔️ Parasailing, jet skiing, banana boat rides (optional)
Afternoon: Return to Pattaya, visit Nong Nooch Tropical Garden - cultural shows & elephant performances
Evening: Tiffany's Cabaret Show, one of the best in Thailand!
Hotel: 4-star beach resort in Pattaya
📅DAY 4 (April 13, 2025) | Pattaya - Bangkok - Floating Market 🚤
Morning: Return to Bangkok, stop by:
✔️ Damnoen Saduak Floating Market, explore traditional Thai boats & street food
✔️ Maeklong Railway Market, watch the train pass through the bustling market
Afternoon: Visit Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn) and shopping at MBK Mall
Evening: Free time to explore Bangkok's night markets
Hotel: 4-star hotel in Bangkok
📅 DAY 5 (April 14, 2025) | Bangkok - Phi Phi Islands (Optional) 🏖️
Morning: Free time OR take an optional day trip to Phi Phi Islands 🏝️ ($70 extra)
✔️ Enjoy breathtaking beaches & snorkeling
✔️ Visit Maya Bay - famous from The Beach movie
Evening: Farewell dinner with traditional Thai cuisine
Hotel: 4-star hotel in Bangkok
📅 DAY 6 (April 15, 2025) | Bangkok → Ho Chi Minh City ✈️
Morning: Free time for last-minute shopping at Platinum Fashion Mall
Afternoon: Transfer to airport, fly back to Ho Chi Minh City
🏨 Accommodation:
✔️ 2 nights in Bangkok - 4-star city hotel
✔️ 2 nights in Pattaya - 4-star beach resort
✔️ 1 night in Bangkok (final night)

📌 Why Choose This Tour?
✅ All-inclusive package - Flights, hotels, meals & entrance fees included
✅ Vietnamese-speaking tour guide for easy communication
✅ Flexible itinerary with optional activities

✨ KOREA DISCOVERY TOUR - 6 DAYS 5 NIGHTS ✨

🌏 Departure from: Ho Chi Minh City
📅 Tour Date: April 10 - April 15, 2025
💰 Price: $799/person - All-inclusive package!

✨ Highlights:
✅ Seoul - The heart of South Korea with palaces, shopping & K-pop culture
✅ Nami Island - Famous for its breathtaking scenery from Winter Sonata
✅ Everland - One of the biggest and best amusement parks in Asia
✅ Busan - Stunning beaches and unique coastal temples
✅ Jeju Island (Optional) - Paradise with waterfalls, cliffs, and breathtaking views

🌟 Tour Itinerary

📅 DAY 1 (April 10, 2025) | Hanoi → Seoul ✈️
🛫 Morning: Flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Seoul (Incheon Airport)
🏰 Afternoon: Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace, experience the Royal Guard Changing Ceremony
🌃 Evening: Explore Myeongdong Night Market - famous for street food & shopping
🏨 Hotel: 4-star hotel in Seoul
📅 DAY 2 (April 11, 2025) | Seoul - Nami Island - Petite France 🍂
⛵ Morning: Visit Nami Island, famous for its scenic tree-lined paths (Winter Sonata filming location)
🏡 Afternoon: Explore Petite France, a charming French-style village
🍖 Evening: Return to Seoul, enjoy a traditional Korean BBQ dinner
🏨 Hotel: 4-star hotel in Seoul
📅 DAY 3 (April 12, 2025) | Everland Theme Park 🎢
🎡 Morning to Afternoon: Enjoy a full day at Everland - thrilling rides, animal safari & themed gardens
🏙️ Evening: Visit Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP), a futuristic architectural landmark
🏨 Hotel: 4-star hotel in Seoul
📅 DAY 4 (April 13, 2025) | Seoul - Busan (High-speed KTX Train) 🚄
🚅 Morning: Take a KTX bullet train to Busan (~2.5 hours)
🛕 Afternoon: Visit Haedong Yonggungsa Temple, a stunning temple by the sea
🏖️ Evening: Explore Haeundae Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in Korea
🏨 Hotel: 4-star hotel in Busan
📅 DAY 5 (April 14, 2025) | Busan - Gamcheon Village - Return to Seoul 🎨
🎭 Morning: Walk through the colorful streets of Gamcheon Culture Village
🚄 Afternoon: Return to Seoul, visit Insadong for traditional Korean crafts & souvenirs
🌆 Evening: Free time or optional visit to N Seoul Tower for a panoramic city view
🏨 Hotel: 4-star hotel in Seoul
📅 DAY 6 (April 15, 2025) | Seoul → Hanoi ✈️
🛍️ Morning: Free time for last-minute shopping at Hongdae Shopping Street
🛫 Afternoon: Transfer to the airport, fly back to Ho Chi Minh City
🏨 Accommodation:
✔️ 3 nights in Seoul - 4-star city hotel 🏨
✔️ 1 night in Busan - 4-star hotel near the beach 🏖️
✔️ 1 night in Seoul (final night) 🏨

📌 Why Choose This Tour?
✅ All-inclusive package - Flights, hotels, meals & entrance fees included ✈️🏨🍜
✅ Vietnamese-speaking tour guide for easy communication 🗣️
✅ Flexible itinerary with optional activities 🎉
